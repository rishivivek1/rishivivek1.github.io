    Vivek Kumar

    Most of my time on this planet has gone into telling computer systems how to behave — making them run efficiently and reliably for humans.

    A few years in Industry, I realized humans are far more complex than machines. Since then, I’ve been exploring psychology, history, business, and anything else that sparks curiosity.

    That doesn’t mean I’ve left tech — it’s still my first love and how I pay the bills.

    I write subtle, often satirical pieces about technology, human behavior, and whatever else catches my attention.

    If that sounds interesting, subscribe to my Newsletter — I promise it’ll be worth your time.
